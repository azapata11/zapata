About Me

Thanks for visiting my website! I’m a first-generation college graduate from Puerto Rico, pivoting into the technical writing industry. I’ve spent years working in education and found much gratification in teaching youth and helping fortify their minds. I’ve been looking forward to expanding my horizons in technical documentation. Writing and educating have always been a great passion of mine. Combining the two to help create documentation and presentations that concisely and effectively convey meaning to a user/reader is a perfect fit for the skillset I've developed.

I’ve managed to create this website using HTML and CSS to help give a guide into my readiness to pick up new skills and learn quickly as an asset for any company to help create material. Here, you’ll find some sample documentation I have made, as well as some essays from my time in college under my portfolio section to help demonstrate my skills.

A little bit about me: I am a mother to my dog Yogi (who just turned one!), I love going outdoors, getting fresh air, exercising, concerts, and playing video games with my family. I look forward to learning more about you if you find me a good fit. Thank you, and have a wonderful day!
